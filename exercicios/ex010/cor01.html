The Cosmere

What is The Cosmere?

Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere is a vast interconnected universe that spans multiple series and standalone novels, all taking place within the same cosmological framework. The Cosmere features a variety of planets, magic systems, and civilizations, all connected by a common mythology and underlying physics.

Which books are in the Cosmere?

Mistborn

This series takes place on the world of Scadrial, where individuals known as Allomancers and Feruchemists can manipulate metals to gain extraordinary abilities. The series follows a group of rebels as they attempt to overthrow a tyrannical government.

Stormlight Archive

Set on the world of Roshar, this series follows a group of knights known as Radiants, who bond with magical beings called spren to gain incredible powers. The series is known for its complex worldbuilding, intricate magic system, and epic storytelling.

Elantris

This standalone novel takes place on the world of Sel, where a magical city known as Elantris has fallen into ruin and its inhabitants cursed with a terrible affliction. The story follows three characters as they attempt to unravel the mystery of the curse and restore Elantris to its former glory.
